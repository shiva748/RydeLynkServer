To view and accept offers from operators, navigate to the Orders section and select the booking you are interested in. You will see a list of offers from various operators. You can choose to accept or reject offers from this list.

Once you accept an offer, you will receive the driver's and cab's contact details. You can use this information to get in touch with them directly.

To cancel a booking, go to the Bookings section, select the ride you wish to cancel, and tap on Cancel Booking. Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation process.
